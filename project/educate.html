E-ducate

E-ducate aims to helping university teachers and students to quickly and efficiently switch their working and learning styles to adapt to the new norm of distance learning.

Background

To halt the spreading of the coronavirus, social distancing is implemented in many countries. Not only students but also staffs working in higher education had to make a quick switch to online education. This all while studies have warned that online education can harm student performance, especially students who are already struggling.

Analysis

We will go through different standard steps of the design cycle in order to improve the online studying experience of college students in higher education.

We conducted the semi-structure interview with eight potential users and analysis the data with affinity diagram. As showing in the graph, the feedback from interview were noted down on digital sticky notes. Then the sticky notes were coded and grouped to generate some insights. The interest points and pain points were clusterd into the themes including motivation lacking and interaction missing.
