E-ducate

To halt the spreading of the coronavirus, social distancing is implemented in many countries. Not only students but also staffs working in higher education had to make a quick switch to online education. This all while studies have warned that online education can harm student performance, especially students who are already struggling.

We will go through different steps of the design cycle in order to improve the online studying experience of college students in higher education.

As a prerequisite for all analysis work, we do some preparation work with our data gathered from the empathizing part, that is, notes of 8 semi-structured interviews. The notes were filled into the question list thus the individual interview records for every interviewee is generated. All of our members familiarize ourselves with the data by browsing the records.

And the affinity diagram making procedure was carried out with MIRO. Then we use codes to summarize many categories for our sticky notes. Around 20 codes were developed at first, and 23 codes in the end after several times combining and re-organization with theme adjustments.

Then we conducted a usability test with 5 users who also took part in the previous interview. The inVision prototype which used in the test is attached below.
