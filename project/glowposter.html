Signage for Glow Festival

The signage illustration show the key idea for the installation to help visitor understand even without the text.

I was responsible for signages for all installations in Glow 2021, it was quite fun and exciting! With lots of discussion with artist teams we found the core interesting point of the installations and translated them into visual. In total there were 35 posters I made hanging in the GLOW festival. Glad that seeing them helping the visitors to feel the soul of the projects!
